Delete Your Account – Alison's: Booking App

We respect your privacy and give you full control over your account and data. If you no longer wish to use Alison's: Booking App, you can delete your account at any time from within the app.

🔧 Steps to Delete Your Account:

  1. Open Alison's: Booking App on your device.
  2. Go to the Profile page.
  3. Tap on “Delete Account”.
  4. Confirm when prompted “Do you really want to delete your account?”
  5. If you press Yes, you'll be asked to re-authenticate.
  6. Once verified, your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted.

🗑️ What Data Will Be Deleted:

  • Your email or phone number
  • Personal profile data
  • Booking history or related records

🗂️ What May Be Retained:

We may retain anonymized diagnostic data that cannot be linked to you.
